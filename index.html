02

O Problema

PUBLIC GOODS PROJECTS NEED AUTONOMY

Government dependency and bureaucracy hinder social organizations' autonomy, adaptability, and operational efficiency.

ACTIVISTS WANT TRANSPARENCY

Donation processes lacking transparency compromise security, while bureaucracy impedes collaboration with causes, leading to ineffective impact due to a lack of collaborative efforts.

ARTISTS NEED RECOGNITION

Limited visibility hampers growth and recognition opportunities, while securing sufficient funding for creative projects poses challenges for many artists.

03

A creative solution in crowdfunding

ARTIVIST is a web3 crowdfunding platform that transforms collaboration between artists, activists and social organizations.

Our goal is to use web3 technology to revolutionize funding methods for non-profit organizations, artists and for all stakeholders.

04

O DApp

1

A safe and transparent environment

Built on Solana blockchain, our platform ensures fast transactions, minimal fees, and scalability.

2

Campaigns with NFTs

Campaigns will distribute resources automatically, raising funds for NGOs and remunerating artists through their copyrights. Integration with Underdog Protocol allows campaigns to be created with many configuration possibilities.

3

Easy participation

Integration with Stripe will allow contributions to be made using FIAT money, making it easier to onboard new users to Solana.
